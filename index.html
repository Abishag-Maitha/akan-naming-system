Akan Names

This site helps people find their Akan Names by entering their Date of Birth and Gender.

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person.

These children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

Enter your Date of Birth to get your Akan Name
